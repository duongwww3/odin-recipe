Lasagna

Descriptions

Rice and eggs, and any other leftovers can be found in the fridge, they somehow make one of the best dishes known to asians and the world.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Carrot

Steps

  1. Pre-fry the rice: Oil the pan and fry at medium heat for 7 minutes
  2. Crack the egg(s) and beat with salt and fish sauce:Add enough salt and fish sauce for the rice also
  3. Mix the egg(s) with the rice and turn up the heat: Stir and add fish sauce and soy sauce.
  4. Dice the carrot and stir them in: Add the carrot, turn the heat down and put the lid on
